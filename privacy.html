broki's music bot - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how broki's music bot ("the Bot") handles and protects your privacy. The Bot collects limited information about the guild and user ID for validating vote bot data from top.gg.

Data Collection

The Bot collects and stores the following information:

  • Guild Information: The Bot collects basic information about the guild, such as guild name and ID, for internal use and functionality of the bot.
  • User ID: The Bot collects and stores the user ID for the purpose of validating vote bot data from top.gg, ensuring accurate vote counts and rewards.

Data Usage

The information collected by the Bot is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Internal Use: The guild information is used for the proper functioning of the bot's features and services within the Discord platform.
  • Vote Validation: The user ID is used to validate votes from top.gg, ensuring fairness in vote counts and rewarding users accordingly.

Data Protection

We take appropriate measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of the collected data. Access to the data is restricted to authorized personnel only, and we implement technical safeguards to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

Data Retention

The collected data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Once the data is no longer required, it is securely deleted.

Third-Party Services

The Bot may interact with third-party services, such as top.gg, for vote validation. Please review the privacy policies of these services for information on their data collection and processing practices.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. It is your responsibility to review this page periodically for any changes.
